## Priority Framework

We prioritize features based on three factors:

1. **User Impact** — How many users benefit, and how much?
2. **Technical Dependencies** — What must be built first so other things can work?
3. **Business Value** — Revenue, retention, competitive advantage, or contractual obligation?

**Priority levels:**

| Level | Meaning | What It Means in Practice |
|-------|---------|--------------------------|
| **P0** | Must have for launch | The product does not work without this |
| **P1** | Important for launch | Product works without it, but is significantly weaker |
| **P2** | Nice to have | Improves the experience but can follow after launch |
| **P3** | Future | Planned for a later release |
