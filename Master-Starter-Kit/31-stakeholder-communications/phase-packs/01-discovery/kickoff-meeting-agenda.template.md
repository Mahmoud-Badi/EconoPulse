## Agenda Details

### 1. Welcome & Introductions (10 min)

- Round-the-room introductions: name, role, one sentence on what success looks like for you
- Meeting ground rules: phones off, one speaker at a time, all questions welcome
- Confirm note-taker and action-item tracker

### 2. Project Vision & Goals (15 min)

- Present the project vision and core problem we are solving
- Walk through 3-5 key goals and how we will measure success
- **Reference document:** `vision-and-goals-summary.md`

> **Plain-English framing:** "Here is the problem real people face today, and here is how we plan to fix it."

### 3. Scope Walkthrough (20 min)

- Walk through MVP features included — what users will be able to do at launch
- Walk through what is explicitly out of scope — and why
- Invite questions and flag any scope concerns early
- **Reference document:** `initial-scope-overview.md`

> **Plain-English framing:** "Here is exactly what we are building first, and what we are saving for later."

### 4. Timeline & Milestones (15 min)

- Present high-level project phases and key dates
- Highlight stakeholder checkpoints — when you will see progress and provide feedback
- Flag known dependencies and risks
- **Reference document:** `timeline-and-milestones.md` / `roadmap-overview.md`

> **Plain-English framing:** "Here is when things happen and when we need your input."

### 5. Communication Plan (10 min)

- How stakeholders will receive updates (format, frequency, channel)
- When and how to raise concerns or change requests
- Escalation path for blockers

| Update Type              | Frequency          | Channel              | Audience             |
|--------------------------|--------------------|----------------------|----------------------|
| Status report            | {{STATUS_FREQ}}    | {{STATUS_CHANNEL}}   | All stakeholders     |
| Sprint demo              | {{DEMO_FREQ}}      | {{DEMO_CHANNEL}}     | Product + sponsors   |
| Risk alert               | As needed          | {{ALERT_CHANNEL}}    | Decision makers      |
| Monthly summary          | Monthly            | {{SUMMARY_CHANNEL}}  | Executive sponsors   |

### 6. Roles & Responsibilities (10 min)

- Who is responsible for what on this project
- Decision authority: who approves scope, timeline, and budget changes
- **Reference document:** `team-roles-responsibilities.md`

### 7. Q&A (10 min)

- Open floor for questions, concerns, and suggestions
- Any unanswered questions will be captured and followed up within 48 hours
