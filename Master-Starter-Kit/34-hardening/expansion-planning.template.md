## 3. Growth Strategies

### Viral & Organic Growth
- **Referral loops:** How can existing users bring new users?
- **Content flywheel:** What content does the product naturally generate that attracts users?
- **Network effects:** Does the product get better with more users?
- **SEO opportunities:** What search queries does the product naturally answer?

### Enterprise & Upmarket
- **Enterprise features needed:** SSO, SCIM, audit logs, SLAs, dedicated support
- **Compliance certifications:** SOC 2, HIPAA, ISO 27001 — which ones unlock new customers?
- **Custom integrations:** Which enterprise tools need integration? (Salesforce, Jira, Slack)

### Platform & Ecosystem
- **API/SDK:** Can other products build on top of this?
- **Marketplace:** Can third parties extend the product? (plugins, templates, integrations)
- **White-label:** Can this be resold under other brands?

### Revenue Expansion
- **Upsell paths:** What premium features justify a higher tier?
- **Cross-sell:** What adjacent products can be offered?
- **Usage-based revenue:** Can any feature be metered? (API calls, storage, seats)
