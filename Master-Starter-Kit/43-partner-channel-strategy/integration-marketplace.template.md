## 3. Listing Content Template

### 3.1 Listing Copy Structure

```
TITLE (max 50-60 chars):
{{PROJECT_NAME}} — [Primary Value Proposition]

SUBTITLE / TAGLINE (max 100-120 chars):
[Specific benefit] + [for whom] + [using this platform]

OVERVIEW (150-300 words):
Paragraph 1: What {{PROJECT_NAME}} does and why it matters
Paragraph 2: What this integration specifically enables
Paragraph 3: Key benefits (3-4 bullet points)
Paragraph 4: Social proof (customer count, ratings, awards)

FEATURES (5-8 bullet points):
- [Feature 1]: [Specific benefit in context of this marketplace]
- [Feature 2]: [Specific benefit]
- [Feature 3]: [Specific benefit]
- [Feature 4]: [Specific benefit]
- [Feature 5]: [Specific benefit]

CATEGORIES:
Primary: [Most specific category]
Secondary: [Broader category]

PRICING:
[Free / Freemium / Paid — match your standard pricing]
[Note any marketplace-specific pricing if required]

SUPPORT:
Email: support@{{PROJECT_NAME}}.com
Documentation: docs.{{PROJECT_NAME}}.com/integrations/[platform]
Status page: status.{{PROJECT_NAME}}.com
```

### 3.2 Description Best Practices

| Do | Do Not |
|----|--------|
| Lead with the user's problem, not your product | Start with "{{PROJECT_NAME}} is a..." |
| Use the marketplace's terminology | Use jargon from your product that marketplace users won't know |
| Include specific metrics ("saves 5 hours/week") | Make vague claims ("improve productivity") |
| Mention the integration points specifically | Describe features unrelated to the integration |
| Address the marketplace's audience directly | Write generic marketing copy |
| Include a clear CTA | Leave users unsure what to do next |

### 4.2 Screenshot Best Practices

| Practice | Rationale |
|----------|-----------|
| Show real data (anonymized) | Placeholder data looks fake and reduces trust |
| Include the marketplace platform's UI around your product | Shows integration context |
| Use annotations (arrows, callouts) sparingly | Highlight key areas without cluttering |
| Maintain consistent branding across all screenshots | Professional appearance |
| Optimize file size (< 500KB per image) | Faster loading on listing page |
| Update screenshots within 30 days of major UI changes | Prevent misleading expectations |

## 5. Category & Keyword Optimization

### 5.1 Category Selection

| Level | Strategy |
|-------|----------|
| Primary category | Choose the most specific category that matches your product's core function |
| Secondary category | Choose a broader category to capture adjacent searches |
| Avoid | Over-broad categories where you will be buried among 100+ competitors |

### 5.2 Keyword Research Process

1. **Marketplace search:** Search for your product category on the marketplace. Note the top 10 results and their titles/descriptions.
2. **Competitor analysis:** List the keywords your top 3 competitors use in their listings.
3. **Customer language:** Survey existing customers who use this platform — what terms would they search?
4. **Integration-specific terms:** Include terms like "sync," "connect," "integrate," "automate" with the platform name.
5. **Pain-point terms:** Include terms describing the problem you solve, not just the solution.

### 5.3 Keyword Placement

| Location | Keyword Density | Example |
|----------|----------------|---------|
| Title | 1-2 primary keywords | "{{PROJECT_NAME}} — CRM Sync & Lead Scoring for [Platform]" |
| Subtitle | 1-2 secondary keywords | "Automate lead routing and deal tracking from [Platform]" |
| First paragraph | 2-3 keywords naturally integrated | — |
| Feature bullets | 1 keyword per bullet | — |
| Tags/labels | All relevant keywords | Use all available tag slots |

## 6. Review Generation

### 6.1 Review Acquisition Strategy

| Method | Timing | Expected Conversion |
|--------|--------|-------------------|
| In-app prompt | After 30 days of active use + positive engagement signal | 5-10% |
| Email request | After integration used 10+ times | 3-5% |
| Support resolution | After positive support interaction | 10-15% |
| Feature launch | When new integration feature ships | 2-5% |
| QBR / check-in | During quarterly business review | 15-25% |
| NPS follow-up | After user gives NPS 9-10 | 20-30% |

### 6.2 Review Request Template

```
Subject: Quick favor? Share your experience on [Marketplace]

Hi [Name],

You've been using {{PROJECT_NAME}} with [Platform] for [X months]
and we noticed you've [specific positive engagement metric].

Would you mind sharing your experience on the [Marketplace] listing?
It takes about 2 minutes and helps other [Platform] users discover
what you've already found valuable.

[Leave a Review →]

A few things that resonate with other reviewers:
- How the integration saves time
- Specific features you find most useful
- How it compares to doing things manually

Thanks for considering it!

[Your Name]
```

### 6.3 Review Response Protocol

| Review Type | Response Time | Response Strategy |
|------------|---------------|-------------------|
| 5-star, detailed | 24 hours | Thank them specifically, highlight their use case |
| 5-star, brief | 48 hours | Thank them, ask if they'd share more details |
| 4-star | 24 hours | Thank them, address any mentioned friction |
| 3-star | 12 hours | Acknowledge concerns, offer to help, provide contact |
| 1-2 star | 4 hours | Apologize, take conversation offline, provide direct support contact |
