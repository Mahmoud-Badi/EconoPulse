## Positioning Statement

**For** small creative and digital agencies **who** manage projects with spreadsheets and scattered tools, **TaskFlow is** a project management platform **that** combines task boards, time tracking, and client reporting in one place. **Unlike** Asana or Monday.com, **TaskFlow** is purpose-built for small agency workflows and priced for teams that don't need enterprise features.

## Brand Voice

**Tone:** Professional but approachable. Like a smart colleague, not a corporate brochure.

**Do:** Use "you" and "your team". Be specific about problems. Show don't tell.
**Don't:** Use jargon ("synergy", "leverage"). Be preachy. Over-promise.

**Example headlines:**
- "Stop managing projects in spreadsheets"
- "The project management tool your team will actually use"
- "Time tracking that doesn't feel like homework"
