> **Product:** TaskFlow

```
Subject: 10 keyboard shortcuts that make TaskFlow feel instant

Hi {{FIRST_NAME}},

You have been using TaskFlow for a few days now. Time to move faster.

**Keyboard shortcuts you will actually use:**

- `N` -- Create a new task (from anywhere)
- `T` -- Start/stop the timer on the selected task
- `/` -- Open global search
- `D` -- Open the due date picker
- `A` -- Assign someone to a task
- `M` -- Move a task to a different project

2. **Figma** -- Embed Figma files directly in tasks. Designers can
   share work-in-progress without anyone asking "where's the link?"

3. **Google Drive** -- Attach Drive files to any task. When the file

[CONNECT YOUR INTEGRATIONS] (https://app.taskflow.io/integrations)

Setup takes about 60 seconds per integration. Once connected, they
```

"Your trial ends tomorrow -- here's what happens next"

### Preview Text

### Body Copy

```
Subject: Your trial ends tomorrow -- here's what happens next

Hi {{FIRST_NAME}},

Your 14-day TaskFlow trial ends tomorrow. I want to be upfront
about what happens.

**If you upgrade to Pro ($12/user/month):**

Nothing changes. Every project, task, time entry, automation,
and report you have created stays exactly where it is. You keep
full access to everything.

**If you do not upgrade:**

Your account moves to the Free plan. Here is what you lose:

- Task automations stop running (your recurring tasks will not
  create themselves anymore).
- Client reporting becomes view-only (no new reports).
- Integrations (Slack, Figma, Google Drive) disconnect.
- Time tracking is limited to 3 projects.
- You lose access to Timeline and Calendar views.

Your data is never deleted. If you upgrade later, everything
comes back exactly as you left it.

**Here is what I recommend:**

If TaskFlow has saved your team even one hour per week over
this trial, the Pro plan pays for itself. For a 10-person team,
that is $120/month -- less than the cost of one billable hour
at most agencies.

[UPGRADE TO PRO] (https://app.taskflow.io/billing/upgrade)

If now is not the right time, that is genuinely okay. You can
stay on the Free plan and upgrade whenever you are ready.

Either way, thank you for trying TaskFlow. Building a tool
that helps agencies do better work is why we started this
company, and your feedback over the past two weeks has been
invaluable.

If you have questions about pricing or need a plan for a larger

Sarah
```
