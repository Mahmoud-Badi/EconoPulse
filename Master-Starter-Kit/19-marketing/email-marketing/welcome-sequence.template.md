# Welcome Email Sequence

> **Project:** {{PROJECT_NAME}}
> **Sequence Length:** 7 emails over 14 days
> **Trigger:** New subscriber added to list
> **Goal:** Build trust, deliver value, convert to user/customer
> **Tone:** {{EMAIL_TONE}} (e.g., friendly and professional, casual and direct)
> **From Name:** {{FROM_NAME}} (e.g., "Sarah from {{PROJECT_NAME}}" or just "{{YOUR_NAME}}")

---

## Sequence Overview

| Email | Day | Subject | Goal | Tone |
|-------|-----|---------|------|------|
| 1 | Day 0 (Immediate) | Welcome + deliver promise | Deliver lead magnet, set expectations | Warm, appreciative |
| 2 | Day 1 | Quick win / immediate value | Prove your value with one actionable tip | Helpful, practical |
| 3 | Day 3 | Story / why this exists | Build emotional connection through origin story | Personal, vulnerable |
| 4 | Day 5 | Feature spotlight + social proof | Show product value with proof | Confident, evidence-based |
| 5 | Day 7 | Objection handling | Address the #1 reason they hesitate | Empathetic, reassuring |
| 6 | Day 10 | Case study / results | Show transformation with real results | Aspirational, concrete |
| 7 | Day 14 | CTA / offer | Make the ask with urgency or bonus | Direct, compelling |

### Tone Progression
The sequence starts soft (welcome, value, story) and gradually gets more direct (social proof, objection handling, CTA). Never start with a hard sell. Earn the right to pitch by giving value first.

## Email 1: Welcome + Deliver Promise

**Send timing:** Immediately upon signup (within 1-2 minutes)

### Subject Line Options (A/B test two)
- A: "Welcome to {{PROJECT_NAME}} -- here's your {{LEAD_MAGNET_NAME}}"
- B: "You're in! Here's the {{LEAD_MAGNET_NAME}} you requested"
- C: "{{FIRST_NAME}}, your {{LEAD_MAGNET_NAME}} is ready"

### Preview Text
"Plus what to expect from these emails (hint: no spam, just value)"

### Body Copy Template

```
Subject: Welcome to {{PROJECT_NAME}} -- here's your {{LEAD_MAGNET_NAME}}

Hi {{FIRST_NAME}},

Welcome! I'm {{YOUR_NAME}}, the founder of {{PROJECT_NAME}}.

First things first -- here's the {{LEAD_MAGNET_NAME}} I promised:

[DOWNLOAD/ACCESS {{LEAD_MAGNET_NAME}}] ({{LEAD_MAGNET_URL}})

I created this because {{REASON_LEAD_MAGNET_EXISTS}} -- and I hope
it saves you {{TIME_OR_EFFORT_SAVED}}.

Quick heads up on what to expect from me:

- I'll send you {{FREQUENCY}} emails ({{DAY_OF_WEEK}} usually)
- Every email will include {{VALUE_PROMISE}} (tips, insights, resources)
- I will occasionally share updates about {{PROJECT_NAME}}
- You can unsubscribe anytime (but I hope you won't want to)

Over the next two weeks, I'll share some of my best insights on
{{TOPIC}} -- things that took me {{TIMEFRAME}} to learn.

Tomorrow, I'll send you a quick tip that {{BENEFIT_OF_TIP}}.

Talk soon,
{{YOUR_NAME}}

P.S. Hit reply and tell me -- what's your biggest challenge with
{{PROBLEM_DOMAIN}} right now? I read every response.
```

### CTA Button Text
"Download {{LEAD_MAGNET_NAME}}"

### Notes
- Deliver the promise FIRST, before any other content
- Set expectations for future emails (frequency, content type)
- The P.S. reply request is critical: replies signal to email providers that your emails are wanted, improving deliverability
- Keep this email short and focused

- Include urgency but keep it real (actual deadline, not fake scarcity)
- Soften the close: acknowledge they might not be ready, and that is okay
- Transition them to your regular newsletter so the relationship continues

## A/B Testing Guidance

### What to Test Across the Sequence

| Element | Test Approach | What You'll Learn |
|---------|--------------|-------------------|
| **Subject lines** | Test 2 variants per email (split 50/50) | Which hooks drive opens |
| **Send times** | Test morning vs. afternoon for Email 1 | When your audience checks email |
| **CTA button text** | Test benefit-focused vs. action-focused | What drives clicks |
| **Email length** | Test shorter vs. longer versions of Email 3 | How much story your audience wants |
| **Offer type** | Test discount vs. extended trial in Email 7 | What motivates conversion |
| **P.S. content** | Test question vs. teaser in P.S. lines | What drives replies |

### Testing Rules
- Test ONE variable at a time (if you change the subject AND the body, you cannot know which caused the difference)
- Need at least 200 subscribers per variant for statistical significance
- Run the test for at least 24 hours before declaring a winner
- Document your results and apply learnings to future sequences

## Transition to Regular Newsletter

After the 7-email welcome sequence completes (Day 14):

1. **Wait 3-4 days** before the first regular newsletter (give them a break)
2. **Tag subscribers** who completed the sequence: `sequence:welcome-complete`
3. **Move them** to your regular newsletter segment
4. **Track conversion:** How many welcome sequence recipients became users/customers?
5. **Re-engage non-openers:** If someone did not open any welcome emails, send a re-engagement email before moving them to the newsletter
