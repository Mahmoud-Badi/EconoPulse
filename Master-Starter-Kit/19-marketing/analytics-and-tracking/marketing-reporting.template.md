## Metrics Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **MRR** | Monthly Recurring Revenue — sum of all active subscription values for the month |
| **ARR** | Annual Recurring Revenue — MRR multiplied by 12 |
| **CAC** | Customer Acquisition Cost — total marketing and sales spend divided by new customers acquired |
| **LTV** | Lifetime Value — average revenue per customer multiplied by average customer lifespan |
| **LTV:CAC** | Ratio of lifetime value to acquisition cost — measures ROI on acquisition spend |
| **Churn Rate** | Percentage of customers who cancel or do not renew in a given period |
| **NRR** | Net Revenue Retention — measures revenue retained from existing customers, including expansion and contraction |
| **ARPU** | Average Revenue Per User — total revenue divided by total active customers |
| **NPS** | Net Promoter Score — % of promoters (9-10) minus % of detractors (0-6) |
| **DAU/MAU** | Daily Active Users divided by Monthly Active Users — measures engagement frequency |
| **Activation Rate** | Percentage of new signups who reach the "aha moment" or first key action |
| **Conversion Rate** | Percentage of visitors/users who complete a desired action (context-dependent) |
| **CTR** | Click-Through Rate — clicks divided by impressions (for ads and emails) |
| **CPC** | Cost Per Click — total ad spend divided by total clicks |
| **CPA** | Cost Per Acquisition — total ad spend divided by total conversions from that ad |
| **ROAS** | Return On Ad Spend — revenue attributed to ads divided by ad spend |
| **WoW** | Week-over-Week — comparing this week to last week |
| **MoM** | Month-over-Month — comparing this month to last month |
| **pp** | Percentage points — the absolute difference between two percentages (5% to 8% = +3pp) |
| **Pipeline Coverage** | Total pipeline value divided by revenue target (aim for 3-4x) |
| **Win Rate** | Percentage of deals in pipeline that become Closed Won |
| **GMV** | Gross Merchandise Value — total value of goods/services transacted (for marketplaces) |
