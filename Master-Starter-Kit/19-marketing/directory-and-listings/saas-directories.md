## 1. G2 (g2.com)

### Overview
G2 is the most influential B2B software review platform. Its "G2 Grid" placement (based on market presence and satisfaction) is a widely recognized trust signal. Being listed is essentially mandatory for any B2B SaaS product.

### Step-by-Step Submission

1. **Create a vendor account** at https://seller.g2.com
2. **Claim or create your product profile**:
   - Search for {{PROJECT_NAME}} (it may already exist from user-generated data)
   - If it exists, claim it. If not, click "Add a Product"
3. **Complete your profile**:
   - Product name: {{PROJECT_NAME}}
   - Website URL: {{WEBSITE_URL}}
   - Company description: 150-300 words, keyword-rich but natural
   - Logo: 200x200px minimum, PNG with transparent background preferred
   - Screenshots: 3-5 high-quality product screenshots (1200x800px minimum)
   - Video: Optional but highly recommended (product demo, 1-3 minutes)
   - Pricing: Add pricing tiers (or mark as "Contact for pricing")
   - Features: Check all relevant feature checkboxes (be thorough and honest)
   - Integrations: List all integrations
4. **Select categories**:
   - Primary category: Choose the most specific relevant category (e.g., "Project Management" not just "Productivity")
   - Secondary categories: Add 2-3 additional categories where {{PROJECT_NAME}} genuinely fits
   - Subcategories: Select all applicable subcategories
5. **Submit for review**: G2 will verify your product (typically 1-2 weeks)

### Profile Optimization Checklist

- [ ] Logo uploaded (clear, recognizable, proper dimensions)
- [ ] 5+ product screenshots showing key features
- [ ] Product demo video embedded
- [ ] All relevant categories and subcategories selected
- [ ] Complete feature checklist filled out
- [ ] All integrations listed
- [ ] Pricing information added or "Contact for pricing" set
- [ ] Company description is keyword-optimized
- [ ] "What is {{PROJECT_NAME}}" section completed
- [ ] Alternatives and competitors acknowledged (helps with comparison pages)

### Getting Your First Reviews (Goal: 10+ for Grid placement)

G2 requires a minimum of 10 reviews to appear on the G2 Grid. Here is how to get them ethically:

1. **Identify happy customers**: Look at NPS scores, support satisfaction, active users
2. **Personal outreach**: Email your top 20 customers individually (not mass blast)
3. **Use G2's review collection tools**: G2 provides review links and widgets
4. **In-app prompt**: After a positive interaction, show a subtle prompt to leave a G2 review
5. **G2 Review Campaign**: G2 offers $10 gift card incentives per review (G2 funds this, not you - this is their program and is considered ethical)

### G2 Badges and How to Earn Them

| Badge | Requirement | Value |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| High Performer | High satisfaction scores, lower market presence | Great for startups |
| Leader | High satisfaction + high market presence | The gold standard |

| Best Relationship | Highest relationship scores | Shows customer love |
| Easiest to Use | Highest ease-of-use scores | Powerful for self-serve products |

### G2 Comparison Pages
G2 automatically creates "{{PROJECT_NAME}} vs {{COMPETITOR_1}}" comparison pages. You can influence these by:
- Encouraging reviewers to mention specific features you excel at
- Ensuring your feature checklist is complete and accurate
- Having more and better reviews than competitors in your category

## 2. Capterra (capterra.com)

### Overview
Owned by Gartner, Capterra is one of the highest-traffic software review sites. Its PPC program allows vendors to bid for top placement in category searches. Strong SEO means Capterra pages often rank on page 1 for "[type] software" searches.

### Step-by-Step Submission

1. **Create a vendor account** at https://www.capterra.com/vendor
2. **Add your product**:
   - Product name, website, company info
   - Select your primary software category
3. **Complete your profile**:
   - Description: 250-500 words (Capterra allows longer descriptions)
   - Logo: High-resolution PNG
   - Screenshots: **Minimum 3, recommended 5+** (this is enforced)
     - Requirements: 1366x768px minimum, PNG or JPG
     - Show real product UI, not marketing mockups
     - Include annotations/callouts on screenshots explaining features
   - Video: YouTube or Vimeo embed URL
   - Features: Detailed feature list
   - Pricing: Tier breakdown with pricing
4. **Submit for review**: Capterra reviews and approves listings (1-3 weeks)

### Screenshot Requirements (Strict)
Capterra has specific screenshot guidelines:
- **Minimum 3 screenshots** (listings with fewer may be rejected or deprioritized)
- Show actual product interface (not marketing materials)
- Each screenshot should demonstrate a different feature/workflow
- Recommended: Add text annotations or callouts to each screenshot
- Resolution: 1366x768px minimum
- Format: PNG or JPG
- No watermarks or promotional text overlays

### Review Solicitation Strategy

Capterra occasionally runs their own incentive programs where reviewers get gift cards. Beyond that:

1. **Direct link sharing**: Generate your Capterra review link and share with customers
2. **Email campaign**: Send to your most engaged customers (see email template below)
3. **Post-support review request**: After resolving a support ticket positively, ask for a review
4. **Quarterly review drives**: Coordinate a push for reviews every quarter

### PPC Program (Pay-Per-Click)

Capterra offers a PPC program where you bid for top placement in category search results:

- **How it works**: You set a monthly budget and bid per click
- **Cost**: Varies by category ($2-15+ per click typically)
- **Value**: High-intent traffic (people actively searching for software solutions)
- **Recommendation**: Start with a small budget ($200-500/month), test conversion rates, then scale
- **Optimization**: Ensure your profile is fully optimized before spending on PPC

### Profile Optimization Checklist

- [ ] Detailed product description (250-500 words, naturally keyword-rich)
- [ ] 5+ high-quality screenshots with annotations
- [ ] Product demo video
- [ ] Complete feature list
- [ ] Accurate pricing tiers
- [ ] All deployment options listed (Cloud, On-Premise, etc.)
- [ ] Target market specified (Small Business, Mid-Market, Enterprise)
- [ ] Integration list complete
- [ ] Support options documented

### Expected Timeline
- Profile creation to live listing: 1-3 weeks
- Competitive positioning: 20+ reviews recommended
- PPC program activation: Available immediately after listing approval

## 3. GetApp (getapp.com)

### Overview
GetApp is owned by the same parent company as Capterra (Gartner Digital Markets). While listings share some data, GetApp has a distinct audience and ranking algorithm. Its "Category Leaders" report is a separate badge from Capterra.

### How It Differs from Capterra

| Aspect | Capterra | GetApp |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Audience | Broader software buyers | More tech-savvy, cloud-focused |
| Ranking | Reviews + PPC | "GetRank" algorithm (reviews, integrations, mobile, social) |
| Unique Feature | PPC bidding | Category Leaders quarterly report |
| Review overlap | Partial sync with GetApp | Partial sync with Capterra |

### Optimization Tips Specific to GetApp

1. **GetRank Score**: GetApp uses a proprietary scoring system based on:
   - Reviews (quantity and quality)
   - Integrations (more integrations = higher score)
   - Mobile capability (mobile app or responsive = bonus)
   - Social presence (company social media activity)
   - Security features documented
2. **Category Leaders**: Aim for GetApp's quarterly "Category Leaders" report
3. **Separate optimization**: Even though some data syncs, optimize your GetApp profile independently
4. **Integration emphasis**: List every integration on GetApp - it weighs this more heavily than Capterra

### Step-by-Step Submission
1. If you already have a Capterra listing, check if a GetApp profile was auto-created
2. If not, submit at https://www.getapp.com (look for "List Your Software" or vendor portal)
3. Complete all fields, paying special attention to integrations and mobile capabilities
4. Encourage reviews specifically on GetApp (separate from Capterra reviews)

## Managing and Responding to Reviews

### Response Framework

**All reviews deserve a response.** Responding shows you care and influences future reviewers.

#### Positive Reviews (4-5 stars)
- Thank the reviewer by name
- Acknowledge specific points they mentioned
- Mention any upcoming features related to what they love
- Keep it brief (2-4 sentences)

**Example:**
> Thank you so much for the detailed review, {{REVIEWER_NAME}}! We're thrilled that {{FEATURE_MENTIONED}} has made a difference in your workflow. We're actually working on {{UPCOMING_FEATURE}} that I think you'll love. Thanks for being a valued part of our community!

#### Constructive Reviews (3-4 stars)
- Thank them for honest feedback
- Acknowledge the criticism directly
- Explain what you are doing about it (or why it is the way it is)
- Invite them to share more feedback directly

**Example:**
> Thank you for the candid review, {{REVIEWER_NAME}}. You make a great point about {{CRITICISM_MENTIONED}} — we hear you. We're actively working on improvements in that area and expect to ship {{IMPROVEMENT}} in {{TIMELINE}}. If you'd like to share more detailed feedback, please reach out to us at {{SUPPORT_EMAIL}} — we'd love to hear more.

#### Negative Reviews (1-2 stars)
- Thank them (yes, still)
- Apologize for their experience without being defensive
- Offer to resolve the issue directly
- Provide a direct contact for follow-up
- Never argue publicly

**Example:**
> Thank you for sharing this feedback, {{REVIEWER_NAME}}, and I'm sorry to hear about your experience. This isn't the standard we aim for. I'd love to personally look into the issues you mentioned with {{SPECIFIC_ISSUE}}. Could you reach out to me directly at {{PERSONAL_EMAIL}}? I want to make this right.

## Cross-Linking Directory Profiles for SEO Benefit

Directory listings create backlinks to your website. Maximize this by:

1. **Consistent NAP**: Ensure Name, Address, Phone are identical across all directories
2. **Consistent URL**: Use the same URL format everywhere (with or without www, trailing slash)
3. **Link to directories from your website**: Add a "Reviews" or "What our customers say" section linking to your G2/Capterra profiles
4. **Social proof badges**: Display G2/Capterra/TrustRadius badges on your homepage and pricing page
5. **Link between directories**: Some directories allow you to link to your profiles on other platforms
6. **Use UTM parameters**: Track traffic from each directory with `?utm_source=g2&utm_medium=directory&utm_campaign=listing`
