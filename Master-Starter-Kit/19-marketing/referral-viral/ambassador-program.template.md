## Identifying Potential Ambassadors

### Who Makes a Great Ambassador

**Behavioral Signals:**
- Uses the product daily or near-daily
- Has referred others without being asked
- Provides detailed, constructive feedback
- Engages in community discussions
- High NPS score (9 or 10)
- Active on social media in your product's niche

**Profile Characteristics:**
- Has an audience (even small — 500+ followers counts)
- Creates content in your product's domain (blog posts, tutorials, videos)
- Is respected in relevant communities
- Genuinely loves the product (not just looking for free stuff)

### Where to Find Ambassadors

| Source | Signal | How to Identify |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| Power users | High usage metrics | Analytics dashboard — top 10% by activity |
| NPS promoters | Score 9-10 | NPS survey responses |
| Referrers | Already referring | Referral tracking data |
| Community contributors | Active in forums/Discord | Community activity logs |
| Content creators | Mention your product | Brand mention monitoring, social search |
| Support heroes | Help other users | Community and support channel activity |
| Beta testers | Early believers | Beta program participants |

## Program Tiers

### Tier 1: Advocate (Entry Level)
**Requirements:**
- Active user for 30+ days
- Completed onboarding
- Positive NPS score (8+)

**Perks:**
- "Advocate" badge on profile
- Early access to new features (1 week before public)
- Exclusive Discord/Slack channel
- Monthly ambassador newsletter with insider updates

**Expectations:**
- Share product on social media 1-2x per month
- Provide feedback when asked
- Be a positive community member

---

### Tier 2: Ambassador (Active Level)
**Requirements:**
- Advocate for 60+ days
- Referred 3+ new users
- Created 1+ piece of content about the product

**Perks:**
- Everything in Advocate, plus:
- Free premium plan (or significant discount)
- Swag pack (stickers, t-shirt)
- Name on "Community Champions" page
- Direct line to product team (Slack DM, email)
- Input on roadmap priorities

**Expectations:**
- Create 1 piece of content per month (blog post, video, tutorial, thread)
- Refer 2+ new users per month
- Participate in community discussions weekly
- Provide beta feedback on new features

---

### Tier 3: Champion (Top Level)
**Requirements:**
- Ambassador for 90+ days
- Referred 10+ new users
- Created 5+ pieces of content
- Measurable community impact

**Perks:**
- Everything in Ambassador, plus:
- Revenue share (10-20% of referred user revenue, or flat bounty)
- Annual "Champion" event (virtual or in-person)
- Co-marketing opportunities (featured in case studies, co-authored content)
- Product advisory board seat
- Conference sponsorship for speaking about the product
- Custom feature requests prioritized

**Expectations:**
- Create 2+ pieces of content per month
- Mentor new users and advocates
- Represent the product at events or conferences
- Provide strategic product feedback

## Reward Systems

### Non-Monetary Rewards
| Reward | Cost | Impact |
|--------|------|--------|
| Early feature access | Free | High — ambassadors love feeling special |
| Public recognition | Free | High — "Featured Ambassador of the Month" |
| Swag | $15-50/person | Medium — stickers, t-shirts, mugs |
| Beta tester status | Free | Medium — input on product direction |
| Community role (moderator) | Free | High for community-oriented users |
| Conference tickets | $200-500 | High for active speakers |

### Monetary Rewards
| Reward | Structure | When to Use |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| Referral commission | 10-20% of first year revenue per referral | When referral tracking is reliable |
| Flat referral bounty | $10-50 per qualified referral | Simpler to manage than percentage |
| Content bounty | $50-200 per published piece | When you need content volume |
| Affiliate code | Unique discount code, ambassador earns per use | For consumer products |
| Gift cards | $25-100 per quarter for active ambassadors | General reward |

### Choosing the Right Mix
- **Early stage (< 100 users):** Non-monetary only (recognition, access, swag). You need ambassadors who genuinely love the product.
- **Growth stage (100-1,000 users):** Mix of non-monetary + small monetary (referral bounties, content bounties)
- **Scale stage (1,000+ users):** Formal revenue share or affiliate program + premium non-monetary perks

## Ambassador Responsibilities

### Content Creation
- Blog posts or tutorials using the product
- Twitter/X threads about workflows or tips
- YouTube videos or screen recordings
- Community Q&A participation

### Community Support
- Help new users in Discord/Slack/forum
- Answer questions on Reddit, Stack Overflow, or community platforms
- Share best practices and workflows
- Report bugs and friction points

### Referral Activity
- Share referral link with their audience
- Recommend the product in relevant conversations (genuinely, not spamming)
- Introduce the product team to potential partners or customers

### Feedback
- Participate in beta testing of new features
- Complete quarterly feedback surveys
- Attend ambassador feedback calls (monthly or quarterly)

## Management and Operations

### Onboarding New Ambassadors
1. Invitation email with program overview
2. Welcome call (15 minutes) — personal touch matters
3. Add to ambassador channels (Discord/Slack)
4. Provide ambassador toolkit (logos, screenshots, messaging guide, referral link)
5. Set expectations (content cadence, referral goals)
6. First check-in at 2 weeks

### Ambassador Toolkit Contents
- Product logos and screenshots (approved for external use)
- Brand voice guide summary (how to talk about the product)
- Key messaging and value propositions
- Referral link and tracking dashboard access
- Content templates (blog post outline, social post templates)
- FAQ document for common questions they'll get

### Communication Cadence
| Frequency | Channel | Content |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Weekly | Slack/Discord | Casual updates, community discussion |
| Monthly | Email newsletter | Product updates, ambassador spotlight, metrics |
| Monthly | Group call (optional) | Product roadmap preview, Q&A, community building |
| Quarterly | 1-on-1 call | Performance review, feedback, tier progression |

### Tracking Ambassador Impact

Track per ambassador:
- Referrals generated (signups, conversions, revenue)
- Content created (posts, videos, mentions)
- Community activity (messages, questions answered, reactions)
- Feature feedback submitted
- Engagement score (composite of above metrics)

## Scaling the Program

### Phase 1: Manual (0-20 ambassadors)
- Personal recruitment, 1-on-1 onboarding
- Track in a spreadsheet
- Monthly personal check-ins

### Phase 2: Semi-Automated (20-50 ambassadors)
- Application form for new ambassadors
- Automated welcome sequence
- Ambassador dashboard for tracking referrals
- Monthly group calls replace most 1-on-1s

### Phase 3: Programmatic (50-100+ ambassadors)
- Self-serve ambassador portal
- Automated tier progression based on metrics
- Community-led onboarding (senior ambassadors mentor new ones)
- Formal content calendar and submission process
